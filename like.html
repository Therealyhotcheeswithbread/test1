The most expensive Lego designer in history

The most expensive LEGO designer is the Millennium Falcon. The first set of Ultimate Collector's Series "Millennium Falcon" was released back in 2007, and then its cost was $499.99. Today, you can buy a set of 7,541 parts and 10 figures of Star Wars heroes in the U.S. for $799.99.
